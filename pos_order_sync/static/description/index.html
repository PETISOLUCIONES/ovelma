Odoo Pos Order Sync

Synchronize POS Orders to neutralize workload of POS Sessions!

POS Order Sync allows you to synchronize orders between multiple sessions in Point of Sale. It creates a link between multiple sessions such that at one end a seller can create an order quotation and send it to another seller.

Customer experience is one of the most important parts of leading a business; the more ease you will offer to your customers, the more they will tend to buy the products from your store. Hence, you need to look for a systematic approach to offer the best services.
Long queues tend to make chaos and make the customers leave the shopping cart leading to loss of an assured sale. Overloading one POS would also result in inefficient management. Moreover, when you are dealing with multiple POS sessions, it is important to maintain the accuracy without compromising with the efficiency.
Now, you can sync orders of different POS sessions in Odoo. The module helps you to synchronize your Odoo POS session with another so when one POS is far too busy, others can share the load. The Odoo POS user can send POS quotations from one live POS session to another live POS session.
Synchronize POS Orders to offer the ease to your customers

Any glitch in the POS during order processing or bulk handling orders can lead to customer’s queue abandonment. POS order Sync helps to distribute the load between multiple POS for a quick resolution and keeps the queue in motion.

Hence, it ensures that the orders put in one POS can be sent to another in case of an issue with the POS or the POS user to provide better customer experience to the customers.

The order(s) taken in the event can be easily moved to another POS for quick processing. This ensures the line is moving no matter what. Handle and distribute a multitude of orders coming your way.

Detailed Features List

Below is the detailed list of Feature for Odoo Pos Order Sync

  • The module allows the Odoo POS user to send a POS sales quotation from one POS session to another during a running POS session.
  • Any useful internal notes can also be added to the quotations before sending it.
  • The recipient can view the sent quotation and create sales orders from his POS.
  • The status of the sent quotations can also be checked by the sender from his POS session.
  • The quotations generated in the Odoo backend show the details for the POS session of the sender and the recipient.
