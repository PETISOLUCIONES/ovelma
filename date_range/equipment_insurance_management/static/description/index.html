Equipments Insurance Management

Insurance Management of Equipments in Odoo

This app allows you to create insurance for your company equipment and management of renewals of insurance.

Main Features
  • Purchase/Configuration/Settings menu : Insurance Expiry Reminder Days. You are allowed to set reminder days before getting an expiry renewal. So responsible people will get notified based on these settings.
  • Maintenance/Equipment : Create Insurance from Equipment form.
  • Maintenance/Configuration : Insurance Property creation and setup which will be used in equipment insurance form fill up.
  • Allow you to manage renewal of your equipment insurance.
  • Maintenance/Insurances : List of Insurances for different equipment here.
  • Settings/Technical/Scheduled Actions : Insurance Policy Expire Reminder Service.
  • Maintenance/Insurances : Print PDF Equipment Insurance Report.
  • For more details please watch below screenshots and watch video.
